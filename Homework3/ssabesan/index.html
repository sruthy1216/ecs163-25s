This dashboard explores the relationship between Pokémon types and their battle statistics. The bar chart shows the distribution of primary types, giving an overview of type prevalence. The scatter plot allows users to compare HP and Attack attributes, colored by type. The parallel coordinates plot helps users examine how Pokémon compare across six major battle stats. Together, these views allow users to explore how type relates to individual and combined attributes, transitioning from general distributions to multidimensional comparisons.
